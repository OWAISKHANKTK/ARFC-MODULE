FAZAIAN HONOUR CODE

OUR HONOUR CODES




✩ A Fazaian shall "apply" knowledge rather than "know"; shall "do" rather than "willing to do".

✩ A Fazaian shall consider defeat, in a fair contest,far more honourable than a victory earned through foul means.

✩ A Fazaian shall help the people in difficulties and those struck by natural calamities.

✩ A Fazaian shall practice his religion without any prejudice to people of other faiths.

✩ A Fazaian shall never, lie, Cheat, Or steal nor tolerate those who do.

✩ A Fazaian shall value for more a rupee earned than a hundred found.

✩ A Fazaian shall treat others with respect and courtesy.
