ABDUL RAZZAQ FAZAIA COLLEGE MIANWALI

Abdul Razzaq Fazaia College M M Alam Base Mianwali is a reputed educational institution, which provides quality education at affordable cost to the wards of Pakistan Air Force Personnel and those of Civilians residing in the vicinity. The College has established its credentials by showing exemplary performance in the Board Exams. The College is housed in a state of the art building provided with all educational facilities. So far, more than 3500 students have completed their HSSC Level education from this alma mater.
